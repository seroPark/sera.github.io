Penguin's Plight

Penguin Plight" is an interactive experience that raises awareness about climate change’s impact on emperor penguins. Players explore challenges like melting ice, rising sea temperatures, and food scarcity while discovering how human actions can help protect their habitat and ecosystem.

01 GAME OVERVIEW

Penguin Plight – interactive project aims to raise awareness about climate change and its impact on the emperor penguins’ habitat. To address this critical environmental issue, our team chose to highlight the struggles the Emperor Penguins face, as melting ice reduces their breeding grounds, and rising sea temperatures reduce their krill food supply. By exploring the immersive VR environment, users learn about the adverse effects of climate change on Antarctica and the Emperor Penguins, and the actionable steps us humans can take to aid in their survival and mitigate climate change.

02 DESIGN & DEVELOPMENT

Antarctica as a whole is significantly decreasing in temperature causing the ice to rapidly melt resulting in the sea ice seasons being reduced. With this in mind, our team wanted to implement and showcase the critical conditions they're in, as a result of climate change. 03 USER TESTING

Several key issues were identified during testing:

  • Lack of sound effects or interactive elements.
  • Movement was slow and rough.
  • Static objects created an unrealistic feel due to the absence of animations.
  • Limited guidance and prompts left users unsure of their goals.
  • Unrealistic environmental elements, such as trees and fireplaces, detracted from the intended setting.

We conducted a think-aloud protocol with five participants completing four tasks, followed by a questionnaire for detailed feedback. An additional 16 users tested the prototype and completed the same questionnaire.

To address the confusion users faced upon entering the environment, we introduced interactive non-playable characters, clarified task instructions, and added two objectives that highlight the effects of global warming on emperor penguins.

The environment was refined to balance realism and playfulness by removing random assets and expanding the area for smoother navigation.

04 FUTURE WORK

Enhanced Gameplay Features

Adding features such as collecting penguins, timers, score systems, and advanced NPC interactions, like having penguins follow the player, to increase engagement and challenge.

Respawn Mechanism

Implementing a respawn feature for users who fall into the water after missing the ice.

Educational Ending Sequence

Introducing an ending sequence that educates users on practical ways to combat climate change and support emperor penguin conservation.

Expanded Environment

Expanding the environment with additional assets to enhance realism and immerse users more deeply in a simulated Antarctic setting.

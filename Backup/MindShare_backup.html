MindShare
Final Proposal

MindShare is a mobile application designed to connect individuals facing similar emotional and mental wellbeing challenges. It aims to create a supportive, stigma-free platform where users can openly discuss their experiences and access reliable mental health resources. By fostering a sense of social connectedness, MindShare empowers users to share and connect, promoting a more inclusive and understanding mental health community.

Challenges

1.How can we enable individual comfortability when it comes to voicing out about emotional and mental well-being? 2.How do we enable improved accessibility to proper mental health resources for those in need? 3.How do we tear down societal, social and cultural stigmatisation of mental health issues?

Key Objectives

To design a user-centered mobile application prototype that fosters connection, provides accessible mental health resources, and incorporates insights from extensive primary and secondary research, along with user testing.

Solution

Community Engagement Features: Enabling users to connect with peers, engage in group support sessions, and participate in interactive mental health activities. Integrated Resource Access: Providing credible mental health information, self-help tools, and educational content directly within the app. User Experience Design: Creating an accessible and visually appealing interface that prioritizes ease of use and inclusivity for all users.
